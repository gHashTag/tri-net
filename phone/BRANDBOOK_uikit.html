Principles

Monochrome first.
Black surfaces, white ink. Color is a signal — live/stop — never decoration.
Hairlines, not shadows.
Depth is a 1px ring at 10–20% white. No blur, no drop shadows on chrome.
The pill is the shape.
Every button, tab, chip, status is a capsule. One filled white pill per view.
Mono for data.
If it's a number or an address it's mono; if it's a word a human reads, it's sans.

Do / Don't

Do

  • One white pill CTA per screen
  • Mono for every number, IP, and status
  • Hairline rings for depth
  • Green / red only for live / stop
  • Let space do the work

Don't

  • Colored accents for style
  • Drop shadows or glassy blur on chrome
  • More than one filled pill per view
  • Mixing corner radii
  • Sans for numbers
